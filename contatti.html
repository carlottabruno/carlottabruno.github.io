ITIS Mario Delpozzo in Cuneo

From over half a century the beating heart of technical education in the province of Cuneo.

Born in 1959 from the visionary perspective of Mayor Mario Delpozzo (who gave the institute its name), ITIS Mario Delpozzo was created to provide concrete answers to the needs of the local productive world: training solid technical figures ready to enter the industrial fabric of Langhe and Piedmont.

Over the years, it has transformed into a true technological campus, gradually adding increasingly current specializations:

  • 1959 – Foundation and first courses in Electrical Engineering
  • anni '60–'70 – Chemistry and Mechanics
  • anni '80–'90 – Computer Science and Mechatronics
  • 2010+ – Logistics, Smart-Robot, Biosanitary Chemistry.

Today the institute counts over 1000 students, about 100 passionate teachers, dozens of cutting-edge laboratories and a consolidated reputation as a reference point for those who want to combine theory, practice and innovation.

It is precisely here, among desks, robots and codes, that FlickIt was born.
