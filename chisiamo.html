The team of FlickIt

Four students of 4A Robotics who have combined code, design and passion to create something useful and meaningful.

We are a group of four students from class 4A Robotics at the Mario Delpozzo Technical Institute in Cuneo.

For our project, we divided the roles: two of us focused on creating the project's code, others worked on building the website, and one student designed the logo.

The project was then completed through mutual collaboration. We all together verified the program's functionality and ran it through software testing.

The logo was designed based on the video game we created to help people affected by Multiple Sclerosis and ALS.
